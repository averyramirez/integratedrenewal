Neuromodulation

Brush Prarie, WA
1 hr 30 min, $250

Integrated Renewal utilizes Neuromodulation external devices which allow for stress responses impacting the autonomic nervous system to be treated with the latest technology supported by scientific research to provide relief for PTSD, depression, and anxiety. Neuro imaging allows the brain to move from a flight, fright, and freeze state into a balanced recalibration of both hemispheres, aligning the brain's communication, to provide rest and healing.

Visit our Neuromodulation tab to learn more about this technology.

Neuromodulation requires four sessions on consecutive days which average 40-60 minutes. This is followed by a fifth session scheduled three weeks later to complete the integration period.
