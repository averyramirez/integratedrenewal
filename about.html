Vision and Purpose

Integrated Renewal exists to provide support and care for individuals experiencing life’s challenging seasons. We specialize in walking alongside those experiencing interpersonal struggles, depression, anxiety, post-traumatic stress, insomnia, and grief. Spiritual integration, evidenced-based counseling practices, Neuromodulation, and Safe & Sound are utilized for holistic care.

Integrated Renewal’s unique trauma modalities and 360 approach sets it apart from other counseling offices. In the past year, over 95 percent of our clients reported effectiveness in the holistic therapies received. One client stated, "Thank you for what you do. I no longer struggle with anxiety and depression. I had previously tried several counseling options and did not feel I had made any progress. In just a few months, I have relief from my symptoms, and I am off my medication."
