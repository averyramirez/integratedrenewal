Neuromodulation

Acoustic brainwave neuromodulation uses sound waves to influence neural oscillations, mirroring the brain, and stimulating the autonomic nervous system. Neuromodulation therapy treatments result in greater neuroplasticity and relief for PTSD, depression, anxiety, and insomnia.
