First Responders / Military

First responders are increasingly experiencing a lack of support while seeking to protect our citizens. The trauma they incur has lasting impact not only on them personally but also on their families. We are able to provide support through neuromodulation therapy, which has been shown to have a 93% success rate for those experiencing PTSD, TBIs, depression, anxiety, and insomnia.
